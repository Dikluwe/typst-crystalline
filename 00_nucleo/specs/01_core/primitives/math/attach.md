# 🧬 Crystal Facet: math/attach.rs

> **Crystal Face**: The Attachment Algebra — Subscript/Superscript Binding.

---

## 💎 Facet DNA

$$
\text{Attach}(\text{base}, \text{sub}^?, \text{sup}^?)
$$

**attach.rs** defines the **Attachment Algebra** — subscripts and superscripts attached to base elements.

---

## Prescriptive Axioms

### Axiom I: Position Binding

$$
\text{base}^{\text{top}}_{\text{bottom}}
$$

Attachments bind to **top** (superscript) or **bottom** (subscript) positions.

---

### Axiom II: Limits Mode

$$
\text{limits} \Rightarrow \text{above/below (display)}
$$

In limits mode, attachments appear **above/below** rather than to the side.
