## Prescriptive Axioms

### Axiom I: Geometric Signatures

$$
\text{Signature} \in \{\text{Atomic}, \text{Fragmentable}, \text{Linear}, \text{Anchored}, \text{Meta}\}
$$

Elements are classified by their **dimensional demand**:

| Signature | Demand | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| `Atomic` (Single) | Indivisible | Must fit in one region |
| `Fragmentable` (Multi) | Divisible | Can span multiple regions |
| `Linear` (Line) | Inline | Participates in paragraph flow |
| `Anchored` (Placed) | Positional | Requires specific placement |
| `Meta` (Tag) | Zero-dimensional | No spatial footprint |

---

### Axiom II: Spill Continuity

$$
\text{Fragmentable}_{spill} \Rightarrow \text{continue in next manifold}
$$

Fragmentable elements that exceed region capacity produce **spill** that continues in subsequent regions.
