## Prescriptive Axioms

### Axiom I: Column Unification

$$
\text{Frame} = \bigcup_{c \in \text{Columns}} \text{translate}(c, \Delta x_c)
$$

Columns are **unified** via horizontal translation into a single frame coordinate system.

---

### Axiom II: Footnote Aggregation

$$
\text{Footnotes}_{all} \xrightarrow{\text{collect}} \text{Frame}_{bottom}
$$

Footnotes from all columns are **aggregated** at the unified frame bottom.

---

### Axiom III: Line Number Emission

$$
\forall \text{line}: \text{emit}(\text{LineNumber}, \text{margin})
$$

Line numbers are **emitted** to the margin zone during composition.
