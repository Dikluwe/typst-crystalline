# 🧬 Crystal Facet: foundations/ops.rs

> **Crystal Face**: The Domain Convergence — Logical Symmetries Between Values.

---

## 💎 Facet DNA

$$
\text{Op} : \text{Domain}_A \times \text{Domain}_B \to \text{Domain}_C
$$

**ops.rs** defines **Domain Convergence Laws** — operators as logical symmetries between value domains.

---

## Prescriptive Axioms

### Axiom I: Symmetry Principle

$$
a + b \equiv b + a \quad \text{(commutative symmetry)}
$$

Operators express **logical symmetries** between values, not function calls.

---

### Axiom II: Domain Convergence

$$
\text{Int} \oplus \text{Float} \to \text{Float}
$$

When domains differ, they **converge** to a common superdomain.

---

### Axiom III: Operator Categories

$$
\text{Op} \in \{\text{Arithmetic}, \text{Relational}, \text{Logical}, \text{Compositional}\}
$$

Operators are classified by their **symmetry type**:
- **Arithmetic**: Numeric magnitude operations
- **Relational**: Ordering comparisons
- **Logical**: Boolean algebra
- **Compositional**: Sequence/string joining
