# 🧬 Crystal Domain: foundations/content/

> **Crystal Face**: The Semantic Flow — Linear Composition of Atoms.

---

## 💎 Domain DNA

$$
\text{Content} = \bigoplus_{i} \text{Atom}_i
$$

**content/** defines the **Semantic Flow** — the linear composition of atoms into a fragment of documentary reality.

---

## Prescriptive Axioms

### Axiom I: Atom Sequence

$$
\text{Content} = a_1 + a_2 + \ldots + a_n
$$

Content is an **ordered sequence** of semantic atoms.

---

### Axiom II: Domain Promotion Law

$$
\text{Str} \xrightarrow{\text{promote}} \text{Text} \subset \text{Content}
$$

**Domain Promotion Law**: Primitive entities (Strings) are **promoted** to atoms (Text) to coexist in the content geometry.

---

### Axiom III: Style Envelope

$$
\text{styled}(C, S) = C \text{ in style context } S
$$

Content can be **enveloped** in a style context.

---

### Axiom IV: Identity Persistence Law

$$
\text{physical form} \neq \text{semantic topology}
$$

**Identity Persistence Law**: The physical form of the atom (compact or erased) does not alter its semantic topology.
