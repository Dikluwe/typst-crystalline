## 💎 Facet DNA

$$
\text{op}(\text{name}) = \sin, \cos, \lim, \ldots
$$

**op.rs** defines the **Operator Symbol** — named mathematical operators with special spacing.

---

## Prescriptive Axioms

### Axiom I: Upright Rendering

$$
\text{op} \Rightarrow \text{roman (upright)}
$$

Operators render in **upright** (roman) style, not italic.

---

### Axiom II: Limits Position

$$
\text{limits} \Rightarrow \sum_{i=0}^{n} \text{vs} \sum\nolimits_{i=0}^{n}
$$

Operators may have **limits** above/below or as scripts.
